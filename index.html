About Me

Brief Biography

I have been programming for over a decade. I started way back with learning how to make mods for Minecraft in 2011. I was hooked instantly. I sought out different learning resources for learning even more. I starting making games in Java, since Minecraft mods were made in Java at the time. Then eventually I started using the Unity game engine and learned C# as well as the beginnings of tooling through EditorScript functionality. Finally, in 2020, I came to Godot where I currently spend most of my hobby time. The power of extensibility is something that I really appreciate of the Godot engine. Also the design makes developing games simple and enjoyable. Over the years I have done different challenges to stretch what I was currently capable of. My favourite challenge is Ludum Dare, a large game jam that is hosted periodically. I have been participating off and on over the years as free-time allows.
