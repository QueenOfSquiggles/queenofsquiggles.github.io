# Gruesome Claim

This game was my foray into photo-realism with Godot 4. The game itself relied heavily on third party, high quality assets. The focus on my work on it was to create interesting mechanics.

![some image](/assets/images/gruesome-claim/entrance.png)

The premise of the game is that you play as an insurance investigator checking in on a client who seems to have suddenly stopped paying.

![some image](/assets/images/gruesome-claim/back_hallway.png)

As you explore the apartment

![some image](/assets/images/gruesome-claim/lamp_desk.png)

You find a strange, life-sized doll

![some image](/assets/images/gruesome-claim/clock.png)

Which wouldn't normally be too much of a worry.

![some image](/assets/images/gruesome-claim/couch.png)

That is until it starts moving

![some image](/assets/images/gruesome-claim/camera.png)

There are multiple endings

![some image](/assets/images/gruesome-claim/storage.png)

And a dynamic pathing system used by the doll to hunt you down
